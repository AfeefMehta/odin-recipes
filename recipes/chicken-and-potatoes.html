Chicken and Potatoes

Description

This is a classic dish my Mom used to make when I was younger. I had a huge craving for spice, I absolutely loved eating French Fries, and Chicken was always my go-to choice of meat. So, she had an idea to combine all 3 items into a simple, but very effectively tasty, dish that satisfies your hunger and then some. It's similar to a miniature version of a Thanksgiving Dinner. It quickly captures the heart of the meal with enough chicken to satisfy and enough of your favorite side to add variety. I'd recommend this recipe to anyone. It's very simple, cheap, and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Leg Quarters (any pieces work)
  • White Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Tikka Masala

Steps

  1. Add some Oil, Salt, and Masala to a bowl
  2. Wash the Chicken, dry with a paper towel, and rub the mixture generously
  3. Wash and cut the potatoes, either wedged or dices. Then add them to the tray with the Chicken
  4. Preheat the Oven to 400 deg. F for 5-10 min.
  5. Pu the tray in the Oven and wait for 1 hour. Flipping the chicken and moving the potatoes around 30 min. in
  6. Take out and ENJOY!
